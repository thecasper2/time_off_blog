At the start of 2020 I purchased a Parrot Disco fixed-wing drone from eBay, flying fixed-wing seemed much more exciting than a standard quadcopter drone.

Following the successful flights from this drone I decided to buy a more compact drone, the DJI Mavic Mini.

Below is a feed of all the footage taken from my drones.
